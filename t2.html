Artificial Intelligence

the study of how to produce machines that have some of the qualities that the human mind has, such as the ability to understand language, recognize pictures, solve problems, and learn

Machine Learning

The capacity of a computer to learn from experience, i.e. to modify its processing on the basis of newly acquired information.

Deep Learning

a form of machine learning (= a process in which computers formulate problem-solving strategies by learning from large amounts of data) which incorporates the use of neural networks (= computer systems designed to work in the same way as the human brain)
